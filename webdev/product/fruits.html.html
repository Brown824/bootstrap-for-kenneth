Types of Fruits

Apple

Apples are a type of fruit that are grown in temperate climates. They come in a variety of colors, including red, green, and yellow.

Banana

Bananas are a type of fruit that are native to Southeast Asia. They are a popular fruit due to their sweet taste and convenience as a snack.

Orange

Oranges are a type of fruit that are known for their tangy flavor and high vitamin C content. They are a popular fruit around the world.
